Snapshots — the lion

ℹ What is this page?

The lion guards /data: each snapshot is one checksummed, compressed archive of everything you would cry about losing. Snapshot now takes one immediately; enable the lion service in the YAML to take them on a schedule and keep the last N.

Restore unpacks a snapshot back over /data (the checksum is verified first). Two flavours:

Restore = overlay — brings back what was in the snapshot but keeps files you created since (safe default). Mirror = make /data exactly the snapshot, which deletes anything added after it. Restart affected services afterward, or reboot.
